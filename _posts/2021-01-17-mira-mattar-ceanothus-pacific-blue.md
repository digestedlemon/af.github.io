# <span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">Ceanothus Pacific Blue</span>

<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">Some mornings I wake up mistaking wanting another world</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">with just wanting a fuck.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Inside the correction is a tender hand</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">resting on my forehead</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">not, in its pale heart, secretly wishing</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">for my hair to be smooth</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">so it can be put at an Easter table with the other eggs</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">in a convincing dress.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">I do not resist it</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;but it will not do or undo</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">a civil war, demolition, my mother,</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">bile duct cancer, capital, rape,</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">dopamine imbalances, the suburbs or sickness.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It will not do or undo what was spat in my face</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">or what my face spat back.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The old journals record minor devastations</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">piled up into major ones</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">making up at least one tendon in one arm of the world’s architecture.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You should have seen me cockily</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">storm the ocean as if it were merely a lake</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">until the unlikeliest source said</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">(mixing drinks in Edenbridge Town)</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">‘you were fighting for yourself all along’.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All the he’s glint inside the left brain</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">tucked behind my ear to smoke later.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The shadow forming by the communal bins</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">is definitely not just a fox. Walk backwards into</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">tense and agile fingers would come towards me in the hallway</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">telling me what I was</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">under the Italianate roses</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">of an imagined and indefensible Europe</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;squatting with our goats at the passport office</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">you fuck – here’s my tax receipt, here’s me</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">snuffling for my chicken dinner.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Someone is always getting pushed down the stairs unevenly</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">here or in Worcester Park, there or in a Bangladeshi H&M factory,</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">the plughole of the Amathus Beach Hotel in Limassol</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">was always gurgling with blood.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I had rested inside my equation for a lifetime</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">yet whichever way I balanced it I was dead</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">and so were you and so were the cleaners at the Amathus Beach Hotel.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;D reassured me about truth at the kitchen table</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">so I loosened my grip on knowing. Then</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">could I see that to align yourself or others entirely</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">with conditions and the symptoms of conditions</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">was the same as laying down at 40</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;still waiting to be tucked in</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">while the shadow comes from behind the ear</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">ready to work you dead and</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;you’re on the ground with your body –</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">everything smells metallic,</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">you turn your own mouth over in the dirt</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">where it’s always been</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">muttering:</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">refusal is a kind of devotion.</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Look I’ll still love you if you want to die</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">but meanwhile I can say to the ceanothus at least:</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;oh pacific blue,</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">can’t you see it is only the opposite</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">of really wanting to be alive.</span>

<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span>  
<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 12pt;">Note: The phrase about Europe’s indefensibility comes from Aimé Césaire’s 1950 essay &#8216;Discourse on Colonialism’.</span>
